Scoring. Each segment is worth a points pool = (gainm + ½·lossm + 8·km + 20·log₁₀ efforts) × terrain, where terrain = climb 1.0 / flat 0.85 / descent 0.65. The fastest rider takes the full pool; ranks 2–10 earn 80, 66, 56, 48, 40, 32, 24, 16, 8% of it — and nothing below 10th. An athlete's total is the sum across all segments.
